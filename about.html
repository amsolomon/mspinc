About Mark Solomon Properties

-Florida Property Preservation Specialist-

A leading Property Preservation firm in our territory, Mark Solomon Properties Inc. takes pride helping clean up America one house at a time. We work tirelessly to protect the assets of our clients and future homes of our neighbors.

Initially formed as a small firm composed of only a few people flipping homes, Mark Solomon Properties was eventually incorporated in 2004, in Volusia County, Florida. Over the years with the help of dedicated employees, this firm has grown from a truck and trailer operation to a leader in Property Preservation Services. A firm built on long-term relationships with mortgage servicers, based upon trust and integrity. Throughout Central Florida, Mark Solomon Properties Inc. has now expanded into a multitude of services required to maintain bank owned properties and reduce asset risk.

Our Office is located in South Daytona, Florida with office hours from 6:30 AM EST to 6:30 PM EST. If there are any questions regarding any Mark Solomon Properties' employment opportunities, please feel free to contact us.
