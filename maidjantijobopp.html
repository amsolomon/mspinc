Janitorial - Maid Position Information

  • Position Title: Janitorial Servicer
  • Job Type: Regular Full Time (30-40 hrs/wk, )
  • Compensation: $10.00-11.00 per hour, paid weekly on Friday
  • Job Location: Based out of Daytona Beach, FL
  • Travel Requirements: 100 mile radius around Central Florida
  • Job Schedule: Varies - Monday-Sunday 7am-7pm
  • Required Education: High School Degree or equivalent
  • Required Experience: 1-2 years outdoor property maintenance
  • Required License: Driver's License with Insurable Driving Record
  • Physical Requirements: Lift 50 lbs; repetitive mannual movement

    • Job Description:

    A leading Property Preservation firm in the area we serve, Mark Solomon Properties Inc. takes pride helping clean up America one house at a time. As a Janitorial Servicer, your efforts are what make our client's properties' presentable, sellable and ready to go back on the housing market.

    Day to day, you will be responsible for maintaining the cleanliness and appearance of our client's properties in the Central Florida area. Our Firm needs a knowledgeable and experienced person to initially-clean vacant homes as well as do monthly maid services on bank owned properties. Servicers that excel in this position are able to quickly work and clean, while also photographing their work.

    As per industry requirements, successful completion of a background check is required for this position. Also, all services completed by an employee of MSP must be justified by photo-evidence. MSP management will review all pictures to ensure the job was completed properly, in a workman like manner and in a timely manner. Each individual service will have various job completion, and time period benchmarks.

    Our firm is in need someone that can follow meticulous work-order instructions, maintain attention to details, and is able to commit and adhere to deadlines. As most work is processed via client-websites and web-based applications, candidates must have a stronger ability and aptitude to utilize Internet, email, digital photography and cell phone communication. These technology proficiencies must be present in addition to having the ability and knowhow surrounding the use of standard equipment used for cleaning houses.

    MSP services the counties of Volusia, Flagler, St. Johns, Lake, Seminole, Brevard and Orange. Therefore applicants must have reliable transportation to get to our meeting point, as well as an insurable driving record in order to drive company vehicles around our territory. Applicants must be able to be smoke-free for a majority of the workday. As our clients do not allow smoking on or near their assets, and our firm does not allow smoking in our vehicles.

    MSP is hiring in order to guarantee that our firm has competent people in place to instantly, and consistently service the needs of our clients. MSP is looking for trust worthy, responsible, hard-working candidates, people who can consistently show up to work, getting the job done quickly, efficiently and effectively, while never dragging your feet. In our industry’s environment punctuality is key, communication is vital and the ability for employees to conduct themselves in a workman-like manner is essential.

    Mark Solomon Properties Inc. is an equal opportunity employer and a Drug Free Workplace.
